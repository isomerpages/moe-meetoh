<h3>Chinese Speech and Drama Club</h3>
<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To inculcate appreciation and learning of Chinese culture and inheritance
of Chinese Linguistic Performing Arts by students.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To provide students with Chinese Speech and Drama Theatrical skills that
will develop their potential and abilities in Chinese Linguistics Performing
Arts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To motivate and equip students with life skills and inculcate good values
in them.</p>
<p></p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
