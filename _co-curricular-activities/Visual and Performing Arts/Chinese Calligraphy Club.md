<h3>Chinese Calligraphy Club</h3>
<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To cultivate interest among students in the appreciation of Chinese culture.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop resilience and strive for excellence in mastering the art of
Chinese calligraphy.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To instill knowledge of basic skills and techniques in Chinese calligraphy
and Chinese painting.</p>
</li>
</ul>
