<h3>Robotics</h3>
<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop and provide opportunities for students interested in Physics
and Mechanics to pursue their passion beyond the requirements of the Primary
School Science Syllabus.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To groom students who possess a high potential in the field of Robotics
for international and national competitions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To instill in our members values of Compassion, Kindness (Conscience),
Purity and Integrity as they work harmoniously in teams to master the skills
and techniques involved in constructing and programming robots.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop social and leadership skills in our members through the activities.</p>
</li>
</ul>
