<h3>School Library Club</h3>
<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop students' interest in reading and care for the library.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To apply library and information literacy skills acquired in their promotion
and publicity projects for the library such as poster-making, Books to
Movies!, Diorama and Comics Making.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To participate in activities that leverage on library skills acquired
in a fun and meaningful way - Reading Camp, Librarian's Challenge, Learning
Journey to libraries like Sengkang National Library, Singapore Sports Hub
Library and Singapore Sports Museum.</p>
</li>
</ul>
