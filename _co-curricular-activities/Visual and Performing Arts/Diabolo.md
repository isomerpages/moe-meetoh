<h3>Diabolo</h3>
<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To enrich the Chinese cultural experience as Diabolo is a form of folk
acrobatics that has a long and enduring history in China.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To promote concurrent cognitive and psycho-motor development in students.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To allow students to participate in group training and develop interpersonal
skills as everyone in the group must respect and collaborate with each
other in order to execute performances.</p>
</li>
