<p>Since 2024, in line with our commitment to environmental sustainability,
we have decided to <strong><em>go green</em></strong> with our yearbook.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The publication will be available as a digital flipbook, accessible to
all students free of charge. The physical yearbook will not be available
for sale.</p>
<p></p>
<p><em>Click on the image to view the publication. You will need to enter a password to access the content.</em>
</p>

<p><u>Important Reminder: </u>
<br>In compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and to protect

in safeguarding our students' personal information is greatly appreciated.</p>
