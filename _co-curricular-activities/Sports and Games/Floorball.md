<h3>Floorball</h3>
<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop character building in the students, specific to the values
of resilience, fair play and team spirit through floorball.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop a close-knit culture where students are given the opportunity
to learn from one another during training.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To actively participate in zonal / national floorball competitions so
as to continuously improve and have a growth mindset.</p>
