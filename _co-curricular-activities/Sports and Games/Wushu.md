<h3>Wushu</h3>
<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop each student into a confident, resilient and well-rounded person
through self-discipline and training in Wushu.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To create a culture of excellence by inspiring in each student the desire
to do his/her best, who strives for excellence in Wushu and all other areas
in his/her life.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop a sense of self-motivation in Wushu through cultivating the
students' passion for the sports and through opportunities to lead within
the CCA.</p>
</li>
</ul>
