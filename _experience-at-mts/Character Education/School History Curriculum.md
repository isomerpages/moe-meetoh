<h3>School History Curriculum</h3>
<p>Our School History Curriculum aims to instil in Mee Tohians a deeper appreciation
of values through the inspiring stories of our founder and the school's
journey. The curriculum is supported by two key storylines, which align
with the text and displays in our Heritage Centre:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Mee Toh School History</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Showcases the resilience of pioneers in turning challenges into opportunities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Highlights the collective efforts of our school community in creating
a conducive learning environment</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Our Founder's Story</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Illustrates resilience in the face of a challenging childhood</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrates care for others and active contribution to community and
nation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Emphasises how core values guided the founder's actions</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>School Heritage Ambassadors</h3>
<p>Selected students are trained as docents to guide visitors through our
Heritage Centre, sharing our school's rich history and values. Through
this curriculum, we aim to connect our students with their school's roots,
inspiring them to embody the values that have shaped Mee Toh School's legacy
of excellence and community service.</p>
