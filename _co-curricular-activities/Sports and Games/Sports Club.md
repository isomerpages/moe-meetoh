<h3>Sports Club</h3>
<p><strong>Objectives</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To provide students with a platform to show their competence in various
kinds of sports.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To hone students' skills in games that they are already familiar in.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To provide students with new skills for a sport that is new to them.</p>
</li>
</ul>
